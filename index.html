Mopani Painters is a well established company which specializes in interior, exterior redecoration and roof painting, high pressure cleaning and waterproofing.

Servicing Cape Town and surrounding subburbs, our client base is mainly in the north/west regions such as Bloubergstrand, Melkbosstrand, West Beach, Tableview, Parklands and Milnerton as well as Van Riebeecks Hof, Welgemoed and Durbanville. We have completed a number of projects in regions such as Cape Town Central, Sea Point, Vredehoek, Clifton, Camps Bay, Claremont and Constantia. Clients range from home owners, body corporate and managing agents to builders and property developers.

The owner of the company is Keith Archer who has over 10 years experience with paint and waterproofing manufacturers, servicing the Western cape, Northern Cape and Namibian retail and industrial markets.

Keith spent several years with Medal Paints as a consultant and was later promoted to regional sales manager of the Western Cape. Medal Paints (known to some as Gold Medal) is a manufacturer which specialised in decorative, light industrial and waterproofing products (The waterproofing range being that of Everbond). Medal is a Gauteng based company with factories in both Johannesburg and East London and Warehousing in Durban and Cape Town.

Prior to Medal Paints he worked for Duram Products a Cape Town based company which manufactures paints, waterproofing products, metal primers and wood sealers. With the above experience and in-depth knowledge of preparations and applications the company focus is on working according to the manufacturers specifications.

With a team of experienced painters, Mopani offers quality workmanship and reliability.
