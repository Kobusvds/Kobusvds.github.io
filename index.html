Mopani Painters is a well established Paint Contractor which specializes in interior, exterior and roof painting, high pressure cleaning and waterproofing.

Based in Cape Town Mopani Painters services:

North/West regions: Bloubergstrand, Melkbosstrand, West Beach, Tableview, Parklands and Milnerton including Van Riebeecks Hof, Welgemoed and Durbanville.

Southern regions: Cape Town Central, Sea Point, Vredehoek, Clifton, Camps Bay Claremont and Constantia.

Clients range from Home owners, Body corporate and Managing Agents to Builders and Property developers.

The owner of the company is Keith Archer who has over 10 years experience with paint and waterproofing manufacturers and was involved in servicing the Western Cape, Northern Cape and Namibian retail and industrial markets.

Keith spent several years with Medal Paints as a Consultant and was later promoted to Regional Sales Manager of the Western Cape. Medal Paints (known to some as Gold Medal) is a manufacturer which specialized in decorative, light industrial and waterproofing products (The waterproofing range being that of Everbond). Medal is a Gauteng based company with factories in both Johannesburg and East London and Warehousing in Durban and Cape Town.

Prior to Medal Paints he worked for Duram Products a Cape Town based company which manufactures paints, waterproofing products, metal primers and wood sealers.

With the above experience and in- depth knowledge of preparations and applications the company focus is on working according to the manufacturers specifications and with a team of experienced painters Mopani offers quality workmanship and reliability. Mopani is committed to the use of quality products which are guaranteed by the manufacturers.

We also offer a quality guarantee on workmanship (as stipulated in quotes) strict supervision, clean up of site upon completion as well as prompt and reliable service.

Our pricing is competitive and we offer free quotations which are valid up to 3 months.
